A Serendipitous Sip

In the bustling city of Bangalore, after a long, exhausting day at work, we met for the first time.

Our rendezvous began at a quaint tapri, where we shared a cup of tea. As we sipped, the conversation flowed effortlessly, leading to a shared plate of Maggi. Laughter and stories intertwined as we enjoyed each other's company. Before we knew it, we were savoring golgappas and taking a long walk under the starry sky.

The time spent was as refreshing as a morning dew and what started as a simple tea date evolved into an enchanting connection. We invite you to join us in celebrating the continuation of our journey, a love story that began with a serendipitous sip.
